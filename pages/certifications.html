Deloitte Australia · Forage

Data Analytics
Job Simulation

Completed June 17, 2025
May – June 2025

This simulation placed me inside Deloitte Australia's analytics practice, working through the kinds of problems a junior analyst encounters in the first months on the job. The work spanned two core areas that Deloitte's advisory teams rely on heavily: using data to investigate anomalies and support decision-making, and applying forensic technology to draw conclusions from structured datasets.

Tasks completed

  • Data Analysis — structured and explored business datasets to surface patterns, anomalies, and actionable insights; practised translating raw data into recommendations in the format a consulting team would present to a client
  • Forensic Technology — applied data interrogation techniques in the context of forensic investigation; worked through how analysts use data to support or challenge hypotheses in complex, ambiguous scenarios
Issued by Forage
Tata iQ · Forage

Data Visualisation: Empowering Business
with Effective Insights

Completed May 31, 2026
April 2025 – May 2026

Tata iQ's simulation focused on one of the most underrated skills in analytics: deciding what to show, and how, before touching a visualisation tool. The programme built the deliberate thinking process a senior analyst uses, starting from the business scenario and ending at a visual that earns trust in a boardroom, not just a dashboard review.

Tasks completed

  • Framing the Business Scenario — practised translating a stakeholder brief into a clear analytical question; identified what the decision-maker actually needed to see versus what the data made easy to show
  • Choosing the Right Visuals — worked through the logic of chart selection for different data types and audience contexts; learned to resist the default chart in favour of the most honest representation of the underlying pattern
  • Creating Effective Visuals — built visualisations with deliberate design choices — hierarchy, colour, annotation — that guide the reader's eye to the point that matters
  • Communicating Insights and Analysis — synthesised findings into a coherent narrative for senior stakeholders; practised pairing the visual with the "so what?" that makes it actionable
Issued by Forage
